Online Security

IMPORTANT NOTICE FOR ONLINE BANKING CUSTOMERS

Your security is our top priority. Our online security feature helps confirm your identification when you sign into our digital banking service through a two-way authentication process.

Protect Your Accounts

When someone tries to access an account from an unrecognized computer, they must answer one of your challenge questions correctly. So even if your login ID and password have been stolen through spyware, a phony website or even from your own handwritten note, the thief would be required to answer a challenge question to get access to the account. This would prevent the thief from accessing your account information.

Your banking is even more secure than paper-based banking when you bank online. When you use digital banking, you can detect fraud earlier by reviewing account activity regularly, rather than waiting for a monthly statement in the mail. Security alert enrollment is automatic within digital banking.

Contact our Digital Banking Department with questions at 1-877-621-1776.
